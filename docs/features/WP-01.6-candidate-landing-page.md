# WP-01.6: Candidate Landing Page

**Feature ID:** WP-01.6  
**Parent Epic:** WP-01 (Public Marketing Website)  
**Status:** ✅ Existing (as of Jan 2026)  
**WordPress Page ID:** 229  
**URL:** `/candidates/`  

---

## 1. Overview

### 1.1 Purpose
Provide a public-facing landing page for candidates to learn about opportunities, understand the process, and submit their interest or CV.

### 1.2 User Role
**Candidate:** External job seeker exploring opportunities or submitting their profile.

---

## 2. Page Details

### 2.1 URL
`/candidates/`

### 2.2 Page Title
"For Candidates" (or as displayed on site)

### 2.3 Page Layout
- Elementor-based design
- Hero section with headline and call-to-action
- Overview of benefits for candidates
- Step-by-step process or timeline ("How it works")
- Call-to-action button linking to submission form or application process
- FAQ or info section (if present)
- Contact/support info
- Footer with privacy/legal links

---

## 3. Content Elements
- Headline and subheadline
- Brief intro about the company and what candidates can expect
- List of benefits (e.g., pre-vetted roles, fast feedback, privacy)
- Visual process steps (e.g., Submit → Review → Interview → Offer)
- Prominent button: "Share Your Profile" or "Apply Now"
- Link to privacy policy

---

## 4. Integration Points
- Button or link to candidate submission form (future: WP-02.1)
- Optional: newsletter signup or talent pool opt-in
- Contact form or support email for candidate queries

---

## 5. Design & Accessibility
- Mobile responsive (Elementor/Blocksy)
- Clear, readable text and accessible color contrast
- Easy navigation back to main site

---

## 6. Open Actions / Improvements
- Add testimonials or success stories (future)
- Add video intro or explainer (optional)
- Track click-throughs to submission form (analytics)
- Review/update content for HireAccord branding after rebrand
