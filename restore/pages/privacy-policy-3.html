We process personal data under one or more of the following bases:

  • Article 6(1)(a) – Consent
  • Article 6(1)(b) – Contractual necessity
  • Article 6(1)(f) – Legitimate interests

For special categories of data (if any), Article 9(2)(a) consent will be obtained.

  • Identify and evaluate candidates
  • Match candidates with hiring organizations
  • Conduct technical screening and assessment

  • Maintain internal records and compliance logs
  • Ensure security and fraud prevention

We may share personal data with:

As our entity is registered in India and services EU clients, personal data may be processed in:

Where data is transferred outside the EU, appropriate safeguards such as Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) or equivalent safeguards shall be implemented where required.

Unless otherwise required by law:

Data may be deleted earlier upon valid request.
