Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How do I create an account?

Click the "Register" button, select your role (Candidate, Employer, or Scout), and complete the registration form. Your account will be reviewed and approved within 24-48 hours.

Why is my account pending approval?

We review all new registrations to ensure quality and authenticity. This process typically takes 24-48 hours. You'll receive an email notification once your account is approved.

How do I reset my password?

Click "Forgot Password" on the login page, enter your email address, and follow the instructions in the reset email. If you don't receive it, check your spam folder.

Who can I contact for support?

Email us at support@HireAccord.com. We respond within 24 hours during business days. For urgent matters, include "URGENT" in your subject line.
